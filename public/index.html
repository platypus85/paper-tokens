Paper Tokens Generator

Paper Tokens Generator is a free tool to easily generate and print round or squared creature tokens to be used with RPGs or Tabletop games. These tokens can be easily glued to any round or squared supports like wooden circles, or even better, transparent dome circles or self-adhesive felt pads!

How does it work? Paste, customise, print!

  1. Look for the image you want on the web and copy the Image Address
  2. Paste the link copied in the scroll and press Add Token
  3. Customise your token choosing the shape, size, quantity, etc.
  4. Press Print and save to PDF or send directly to the printer!

...You can add as many creatures as you want!
